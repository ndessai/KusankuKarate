ADULT PROGRAM: All fitness levels are welcome as long as you are fit to exercise. The goal is to improve physical fitness and learn basic to advanced self defense.

CHILDREN'S PROGRAM: 5yrs old and up. Younger children take longer to learn the basics and we take care to help a child progress at their pace. We have two teachers and several older students as helpers.

Program Highlights

  • Self Defense: Block a kick, punch, grabbing and pushing
  • Kata: Hidden techniques passed down through hundreds of years. Good for training and exercise
  • Sparring (optional): Orange belt and up students can spar. It's not mandatory but needed to advance to higher level belts.
